About Me

Hi, I'm Lupu Andrei Iulian, a junior front-end developer with a passion for creating beautiful and functional web experiences. I specialize in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and related front-end technologies, and I'm constantly learning and expanding my skill set to stay on the cutting edge of web development.

My journey in tech started in high school when I had to do a website for a project. Since then, I've stopped working because I had to finish high school and now that I'm done with it I can work in this industry. I'm particularly drawn to projects that challenge me to find creative solutions and push my technical limits. I'm a strong communicator and team player, and I'm always open to feedback and new ideas. If you'd like to chat or learn more about my work, please don't hesitate to reach out!
